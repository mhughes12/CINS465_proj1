Chess Pieces and Gameplay

  • ♙ Pawn:
    • Move one space forward
    • Capture enemy pieces diagonally adjacent in front of it

  • ♖ Rook:
    • Move unlimited amount of spaces forward, backward, left or right
    • Capture enemy pieces it moves into

  • ♗ Knight:
    • Move in an L shape, 3 spaces in one direction and then one space left or right
    • Capture enemy pieces it lands on at the end of its move

  • ♕ Bishop:
    • Move unlimted amount of spaces diagonally in one direction
    • Capture enemy pieces it moves into

  • ♕ Queen:
    • Move unlimted amount of spaces in any direction
    • Capture enemy pieces it moves into

  • ♔ King:
    • Move one space in any direction
    • Capture enemy pieces it moves into
